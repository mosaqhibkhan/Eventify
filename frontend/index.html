About Eventify

Eventify is a student-powered platform designed to make college events more visible and more rewarding. Students can report upcoming events like fests, graduations, fashion shows, and college trips happening around campuses. Businesses such as cafes, clothing brands, hotels, and travel agencies can then post special offers tailored to those events.

The first student to report an event earns points and recognition on the leaderboard, encouraging students to actively share opportunities while helping businesses reach the right audience.

Eventify creates a win-win ecosystem where **students discover exciting events** and **businesses connect with campus communities**.
